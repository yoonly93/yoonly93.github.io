Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Bloom ("the App") values your privacy and handles personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

  • Firebase Anonymous UID (automatically generated)
  • Bean record data (bean name, origin, process, rating, tasting notes, etc.)
  • Bean package photos (when you choose to upload them)
  • App usage records (number of registered beans, number of recipes, recipe run count and last run time, last access time)
  • Bean fingerprint identifier (a de-identified hash derived from entered bean information, prepared for future community features)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service provision: Storing, viewing, and managing your bean records
  • Data synchronization: Syncing data across your devices
  • AI-powered information extraction: Temporary transmission to a third-party AI API (Anthropic Claude) to extract information from bean package images and text
  • Preparation for future community features: Pre-accumulating de-identified bean identifiers (not externally shared at present; separate consent will be obtained at the time of public launch)
  • Service improvement: Analyzing usage statistics (de-identified)

3. Data Retention

  • The App does not currently offer user registration; it operates on a Firebase Anonymous UID basis.
  • Uninstalling the App removes the data stored on your device.
  • To request deletion of server-side data associated with your Anonymous UID, please contact us via the email below.
  • Data may be retained as required by applicable laws.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Data storage and authentication (United States)
  • Anthropic (Claude API): AI processing for bean package image/text analysis (United States)
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (United States)
  • We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

5. Data Deletion

  • Personal information is promptly deleted when no longer needed.
  • Electronic files are permanently deleted using irrecoverable methods.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.
  • Uninstalling the App immediately removes data stored on your device. To request deletion of server-side data, please contact us via the email below.
  • Contact: wildkindground@gmail.com

7. Data Protection Contact

  • Email: wildkindground@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be announced within the App.

This privacy policy is originally written in Korean. In case of any discrepancy between translated versions and the Korean original, the Korean version shall prevail.
