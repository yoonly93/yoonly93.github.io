Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Capa ("the App") values your privacy and handles personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

  • Firebase Anonymous UID (automatically generated)
  • Firebase Installation ID (per-app-install identifier, automatically generated)
  • Email address (when signing in with Apple/Google)
  • Name (when provided by the social sign-in provider)
  • Nickname (auto-generated or user-set, displayed when posting comments)
  • Bean record data (bean name, origin, process, rating, tasting notes, etc.)
  • Bean package photos (when you choose to upload them)
  • Recipe data (title, brew steps, drink type, favorite status, run count)
  • Comment text (when you post)
  • App usage records (number of registered beans, number of recipes, recipe run count and last run time, last access time)
  • Bean fingerprint identifier (a de-identified hash derived from entered bean information, used for community aggregation)
  • App usage analytics (screen views, feature events, device model, OS version, language, country) — processed via Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA via AdMob — only when consented through iOS ATT)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service provision: Storing, viewing, and managing bean records, recipes, and comments
  • Data synchronization: Syncing data across your devices
  • Fraud prevention and duplicate account detection
  • AI-powered features: Temporary transmission to a third-party AI API (Anthropic Claude) for bean package extraction and automatic multilingual translation of comments/recipes
  • Community features: Aggregation based on de-identified bean identifiers, display of shared recipes and comments
  • Automatic migration of data from guest (anonymous UID) to a social account upon sign-in
  • Service improvement: Analyzing usage statistics (Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising: Ad delivery and measurement via Google AdMob

3. Data Retention

  • The App operates on a Firebase Anonymous UID or social sign-in (Apple/Google) basis.
  • Upon account termination, personally identifiable information and photos are immediately deleted. Recipes, comments, and rating data shared with the community may be retained in anonymized form (to preserve statistical/aggregation integrity).
  • Uninstalling the App removes the data stored on your device.
  • To request deletion of server-side data associated with your Anonymous UID, please contact us via the email below.
  • Data may be retained as required by applicable laws.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Data storage, authentication, analytics (Firebase Analytics), Cloud Functions, messaging (United States)
  • Anthropic (Claude API): AI processing for bean image/text analysis and automatic translation of comments/recipes (United States)
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (United States)
  • Apple / Google: Social sign-in authentication (United States)
  • We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

5. Data Deletion

  • Personal information is promptly deleted when no longer needed.
  • Electronic files are permanently deleted using irrecoverable methods.

6. Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.
  • Uninstalling the App immediately removes data stored on your device. To request deletion of server-side data, please contact us via the email below.
  • Contact: wildkindground@gmail.com

7. Data Protection Contact

  • Email: wildkindground@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be announced within the App.

This privacy policy is originally written in Korean. In case of any discrepancy between translated versions and the Korean original, the Korean version shall prevail.
